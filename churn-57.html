More than half your members will leave before their first anniversary. Industry data has pointed to this for years, but most studios still treat churn as a billing problem rather than an experience problem.

It's not. It's a Day 1–30 problem.

"The member who churns in month 3 made the decision to leave in week 2. By the time they cancel, the window has been closed for months."

— The Run Rate

What actually drives early churn

Three patterns show up consistently: members who don't visit in their first 10 days almost never develop a habit. Members with zero social connection to staff or other members churn at 2× the rate. Members who don't use a feature they were sold on in the first month never do.

None of these are marketing problems. They're onboarding problems disguised as retention problems.
